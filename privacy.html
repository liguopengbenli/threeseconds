Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 09, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how ThreeSeconds ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

About the App

ThreeSeconds is a social skills training application designed to help users practice real-life conversations in everyday situations.

The app provides guided conversation scenarios, feedback, and confidence-building exercises to improve communication, emotional awareness, and social presence.

ThreeSeconds is not a dating service and does not facilitate real-world matches. It is a self-improvement tool focused on respectful, appropriate, and positive social interactions.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Basic app usage data (analytics)

  • Technical data such as device type and app version

We do not collect sensitive personal data.
